Social Network

This graph shows a social network of Jifeng's Single friends. Every node is a friend belonging to certain category of races, education levels, citizenship, employment status. The color of the inner circle stands for a probabilty of getting married this year, which equals to the percentage of people in the same category getting married at the same age. curves between nodes stand for connections, we can identify who are more connected than others in his network. Of course, people might link to other people not showing in the graph, but for simplicity purpose, I excluded them.

Hover over the nodes to see details information. Click nodes to see pictures. Double click to cancel picture views.
